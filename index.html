How calculations work & assumptions
  • Mortgage formulaStandard P&I amortisation or IO = loan × rate
  • Year 1 interestExact month-by-month amortisation
  • Gross yieldAnnual rent (after vacancy) ÷ purchase price × 100
  • Net yield (Step 3)Gross rent − PM fees − all outgoings, ÷ price × 100
  • VacancyWeekly rent × vacancy weeks = lost income
  • PM feeApplied to rent actually collected (after vacancy)
  • MaintenanceIf left at $0, auto-calculated at 1% of price per year
  • Negative gearingAnnual loss × your marginal tax rate = tax saving
  • Deductible expensesInterest + PM fees + all outgoings + depreciation (not principal)
  • Depreciation~2.5% of price/year for new properties only (estimate)
  • Stamp dutyState-specific rates, investment property (no first-home concession applied)
  • Other purchase costsFixed at $3,000 (conveyancing, inspections) — update as needed
  • CGT50% discount for >12 months hold not calculated here — see tax advisor
  • Capital growthCompounding annually for chart only — not guaranteed
